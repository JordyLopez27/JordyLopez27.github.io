About Me

Howdy! I am a Ph.D. student working under the supervision of Frank Sottile and Paulo Lima-Filho. I am currently in the job market.

Research interests (not listed in any particular order): toric varieties; computational and combinatorial algebraic geometry; algebraic, geometric, and combinatorial approaches to spectral theory; algebraic, geometric, combinatorial, and categorical approaches to mathematical biology; quadratic enumerative geometry and its applications
