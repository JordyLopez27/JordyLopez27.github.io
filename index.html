About Me

Howdy! I am a mathematics Ph.D. student at Texas A&M University, working under the supervision of Frank Sottile and Paulo Lima-Filho. You may find my CV here. I am currently in the job market.

Research interests: toric varieties and their applications; enumerative geometry and its applications
