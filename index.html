About Me

Howdy! I am a fifth year Ph.D. student working under the supervision of Frank Sottile and Paulo Lima-Filho. I am currently in the job market.

Research interests: toric varieties and their applications; computational and combinatorial algebraic geometry; algebraic, geometric, combinatorial, and categorical approaches to mathematical biology; quadratic enumerative geometry and its applications
